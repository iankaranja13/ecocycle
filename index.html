About Us

Founded with a simple yet powerful mission, EcoCycle was born from frustration with waste management in our cities. We transformed this challenge into a solution that rewards recycling. Our platform connects users to local pickup points, tracks contributions, and delivers valuable rewards for actions that benefit our environment.

Our Key Features

DropPoint QR

Scan QR codes at recycling drop points to log your contributions. Track your recycling history and earn points automatically with each scan.

React Native + QR Scanner

Recycle Points

Earn points for every item recycled and redeem them for rewards. Get points by scanning, uploading receipts, and referring friends.

Blockchain Rewards System

Green Wallet

Manage your eco-earnings and rewards through our secure digital wallet. Daily stats for all your environment contributions and rewards.

Secure Payment Integration

What People Are Saying

You earn points by scanning QR codes at drop-off points, uploading recycling receipts, and participating in eco-challenges. Points can be redeemed for rewards like gift cards, eco-products, and exclusive experiences.

We're currently operating in major cities and expanding rapidly. Check our app or website to see if we have drop-off points in your area. We're always looking to partner with local businesses and schools.

We accept paper, plastic, glass, electronics, and metal items. Each drop-off point may specialize in different materials. Use our app to find the right location for your specific recycling needs.
